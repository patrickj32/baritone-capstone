26.5

26.5 scale guitars have been a point of contention amongst many playes. Some don't consider them baritones or simply call them "baby baritones". Here at The Lowdown, we dont descriminate.

Though, not ideal, short scale bari's can get all the way down to drop G and even as low as drop E (depending on the player). Don't let the numbers fool you. 26.5 scale can hang with the best of them.
